El Concilio Medical Transportation Service (MTS)

The MTS program aims to improve access to health and social care for the area’s underserved and unserved residents, with special attention to the experiences and outcomes of individuals who have needs for the prevention or management of diabetes.

Specific objectives

  • Make a minimum of 6,000 residents of the identified service area aware of the service>
  • Offer no-cost transportation service to community members between 8am – 4pm on weekdays, completing a “circuit” route that includes 11 preset pick-up/destination sites that are served 4 times daily, and providing door-to-door service for disabled individuals
  • Transport an estimated 100 riders on a weekly basis to or from a medical appointment
